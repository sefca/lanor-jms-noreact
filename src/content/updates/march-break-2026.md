# March Break 2026

Please be advised that Lanor JMS will be closed for **March Break** from **March 16th to March 20th, 2026**.

## Key Dates:

- **Last Day of School**: Friday, March 13th
- **School Closed**: Monday, March 16th - Friday, March 20th
- **School Reopens**: Monday, March 23rd

We wish all our students, staff, and families a safe and restful spring break! Whether you are traveling or staying home, we hope you enjoy the time off and come back refreshed for the rest of the school year.

*Reminder: All extracurricular activities and nutrition programs are also suspended during this week.*
