# March Break 2026

Please be advised that Lanor JMS will be closed for **March Break** from **March 16th to March 20th, 2026**.

## Key Dates:

- **Last Day of School**: Friday, March 13th
- **School Closed**: Monday, March 16th - Friday, March 20th
- **Lanor Reopens**: Monday, March 23rd

On behalf of the entire Lanor staff, we wish everyone a safe, restful, fun-filled and memorable March Break!  We hope that our students and their families come back on March 23rd energized and positively approaching the last third of the school year!

*Reminder: Please note that Lanor JMS will be closed during the break, including no programming from PLASP.*
