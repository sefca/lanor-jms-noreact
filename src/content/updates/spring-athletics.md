# Spring Sports are Here!

We are excited to announce the start of our spring athletics season at Lanor JMS. We have a variety of teams for students to join and compete in.

## Tryout Schedule:

- **Boys Soccer**: Monday & Wednesday after school
- **Girls Soccer**: Tuesday & Thursday after school
- **Track & Field**: Every morning at 7:45 AM (All grades welcome)
- **Ultimate Frisbee**: Friday lunch hour

## Requirements:

1. **Permission Forms**: Must be signed and returned to the office before the first tryout.
2. **Proper Gear**: Please bring appropriate footwear and athletic clothing.
3. **Commitment**: Students are expected to attend all practices and games if they make the team.
