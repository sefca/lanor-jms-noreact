# Welcome to our new website!

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our brand-new school website! 

Our goal with this new site is to provide our students, parents, and community members with an easier way to learn about Lanor JMS and stay informed about school events, programs, and news.

## What's New?

- **Mobile Friendly**: The site is now fully responsive and works great on phones and tablets.
- **Improved Navigation**: Find what you need faster with our new menu structure.
- **Events Calendar**: Stay up to date with all school activities.
- **Nutrition Program Info**: Easy access to lunch and snack program details.

We hope you enjoy the new site! If you have any feedback, please feel free to contact us.
